About

Founded in 1989, ROYAL FRENCH IMPERIAL is the 1st in France with 3 Michilin Stars. It is known for revolutionizing the way people eat and enjoy food.

Our 21-Star Chef, Alain Ducasse has resolutely made ROYAL FRENCH IMPERIAL the bastion of excellence of French gastronomy. Join us!!
