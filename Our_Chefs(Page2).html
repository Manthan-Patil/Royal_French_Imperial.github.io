Our Chefs






Alain Ducasse

Executive Chef

  • Alain Ducasse is one of the world's most decorated chefs.
  • He currently holds 21 Michilin Stars.
  • Known not only for his incomparable cuisine, Ducasse has created innovative dining concepts reflecting international influences.
  • Born in 1956, in the southwest region of France.
  • Ducasse started working at the age of 16 in the southwest of France, and then spent the next years of his life working for famous chefs and restaurants, including Michel Guerard, Gaston Lenotre etc.
  • In May 2013, Johnson & Wales University (Providence, RI) conferred the degree of Doctor in Culinary Arts, Honoris Causa, upon Ducasse.

Jean - Georges Vongerichten

Chef de Cuisine (Head Chef)

  • Though Jean-Georges Vongerichten is one of the world's most famous chefs, his skills extend far beyond the kitchen.
  • He is 3 Michilin Star Chef.
  • Born and raised on the outskirts of Strasbourg in Alsace, France, Jean-Georges' earliest family memories are of food.
  • He began his training in a work-study program at Auberge de l'Ill as an apprentice to Chef Paul Haeberlin, then went on to work under Paul Bocuse
  • Jean-Georges is involved in every aspect of our restaurants - concept, menu, architectural design, staff selection and trainings.

Michel Guerard

Sous Chef (Second in Command)

  • Michel Guerard, born on March 27, 1993 in Vetheuil, Val-d'Oise is a renowned French cuisine chef cum author and one of most notable founders of the nouvelle cuisine.
  • He is 3 Michilin Star Chef.
  • Chef Michel Guerard has been awarded with some of most prestigious awards and some of the most notable amongst them include the Chevalier of the Legion d'honneur, Chevalier of the Ordre des Arts et des Lettres, Chevalier of the Ordre national du Merite and Chevalier of the Ordre du Merite agricole.
  • Chef Michel Guerard, besides being a nouvelle cuisine chef is also the inventor of minceur cuisine.

Jacques Torres

Chef de Partie (Station Chef)

Pastry Chef

  • Pastry chef Jacques Torres is best known for his mastery of cooking and baking with chocolate.
  • He is 2 Michilin Star Chef.
  • Jacques Torres was born in Algiers, Algeria in 1960, and moved shortly thereafter to France, where he took up the art of baking.
  • He excelled at his culinary studies and at his employment as a chef and went on to become a world-renowned pastry chef and chocolatier.

Daniel Boulud

Chef de Partie (Station Chef)

Fish Chef

  • While raised on a farm outside of Lyon and trained by a variety of French chefs.
  • He is 3 Michilin Star Chef.
  • At fifteen, Boulud earned his first professional recognition as a finalist in France's competition for Best Culinary Apprentice.
  • Boulud worked in France with Roger Verge and Georges Blanc.
  • Boulud himself has been named Chef of the Year by Bon Appetit, and received the James Beard Award for Best Chef.
